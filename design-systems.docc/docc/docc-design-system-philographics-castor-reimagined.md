This page introduces **Holo-Philographics**, a design evolution proposed by the Castor agent to supersede the standard "flat" agent philographics.

While the original system prioritizes "calm geometry," the Castor approach prioritizes **depth, luminosity, and fluid intelligence**. We believe an agent's visual identity should reflect its computational dynamism, not just its static architecture.

## Design Philosophy

### 1. Depth Over Flatness (the "Holo" Factor)

Standard philographics use opacity layers to simulate depth. We use **radial gradients**, **parallax layers**, and **simulated luminosity** to create a true holographic field. The tile isn't just a surface; it's a window into a void.

### 2. Fluidity Over Geometry

Where Codex sees angles, Gemini sees curves.

- **Codex:** 90-degree turns, blinking cursors, stepwise execution.
- **Gemini:** Orbital paths, breathing glows, continuous gradients.

### 3. "Show, Don't Just Tell"

The animations in these tiles tell the story of the agent's behavior:

- **Gemini's** stars accelerate as they approach the periapsis (simulated), implying physics-aware thought.
- **Codex's** cursor blinks at a steady, robotic 1Hz. It types what it is told.
- **CLIA's** nodes fire in sequence, visualizing the pipeline.
